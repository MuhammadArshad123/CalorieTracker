Why our Website

This website is a quick and easy tool to help you calculate and track your calorie intake. Other tools exist for this purpose, but they are locked behind log-ins and are not as easy to use. Many also fall into the trap of obsessively tracking their calories, to the detriment of their overall fitness goals. This website is designed to be a simple and quick solution to help build better food habits.

Trusted API

We source our information Nutritionix, an online database of well over 700,000 foods which you can search for and learn about their nutrients to support a healthy lifestyle to live to your fullest potential.

Calorie Calculator

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation to deduce the daily amount of calories needed to maintain weight. Our calculator utilizes the user's age, height, weight, and sex to unlock their journey to a healthy lifestyle.

Easy Tracking

Our tracker is simple and intuitive, allowing you to track your food consumption and see your total calories without hassle.
